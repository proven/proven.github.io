CipherShare Applications
End-to-end Encryption. High Efficiency Large File Transfer. Integrated Document Management.

With so much to offer, CipherShare is an all-in-one solution that makes it a snap to:

Securely Manage Your Projects: CipherShare is uniquely positioned to enable project teams to collaborate securely and effectively, around the office or around the world. By integrating document management, security and collaboration in one easy-to-use package, CipherShare keeps your project data safe, now and into the future.

Securely Transfer Very Large Files: Need to transfer very large data, document or media files securely over the Internet? No problem. CipherShare’s integrated, compression, streaming encryption and ‘smart’ upload and download managers means you can transfer gigabytes worth of information simply and easily. And because encryption is built ‘right-in’ to CipherShare, you don’t even need a VPN or other link encryption.

Securely Manage Your Documents: CipherShare’s integrated document management features, including automated version control, make it simple to effectively manage documents at the project, department or organizational level. And because CipherShare can handle hundreds of thousands of documents, you can use it for just about anything.

Securely Store Information on Your Desktop: CipherShare’s encryption doesn’t just protect your information on the server. Everything you’ve stored in CipherShare is protected everywhere, including your desktop or laptop. Which means no more worrying about lost or stolen computers at work, at home or on the road.

Enable Globally Distributed R&D: CipherShare is the perfect tool for distributed teams working with sensitive intellectual property. Simple to use, highly secure and easy to deploy, you can provide a secure workspace to your distributed teams in a matter of hours.

Securely Vault Sensitive Information: Everything stored and shared in CipherShare is secured through AES 256 bit strong encryption, which has been certified by the U.S. and Canadian Governments. That means that once your information is in CipherShare, you - and only you - can access it.

Securely Communicate with Business Partners: CipherShare makes inter-organizational or inter-departmental communication simple, without giving any business partner direct access to your computer network. Just set up an account in CipherShare for each partner. All information in CipherShare is ‘need-to-know’ so your partners only have access to the information they need.

Securely Recover Sensitive Information: If your computers are lost or stolen, getting your information back is as simple as loading CipherShare onto your new computer and logging in. It’s that easy.
