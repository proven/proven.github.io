Download CipherShare

CipherShare is a client / server application, which means you must download two separate components:

1)  The CipherShare Server:  The CipherShare Server can operate on any computer using the Windows Server 2000, 2003, 2008, 2012, or 2016 operating system.  (The non-Server versions of Windows 2000 Professional, XP, Vista, 7, 8, 8.1, and 10 may also be used, but the number of simultaneously connected clients allowed may be limited by the OS.)  The CipherShare Server has a low footprint, with the application using less than 10 megs of hard space. 

Remember that you will need enough disk space to store the documents and files that are being shared, and - although CipherShare is highly efficient - the speed at which CipherShare will transfer information will depend on your available bandwidth.

When prompted for license code information, use the following values.

  • Organization Name: CipherShare Demo
  • License Code: BE3D-7A6B-B25B-8826-E3C2-4A69-E9E2-E500

2)  The CipherShare Client:  The CipherShare Client is a low footprint, highly efficient Windows application.  Each member of the workspace must have a client installed on their computer before they can access the CipherShare server.  Although the CipherShare client itself will take up less than 10 MB of hard disk, the actual disk space used will depend on the volume of material being shared among the workspace members.
